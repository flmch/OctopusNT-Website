Smart Home

――Where dream becomes true

Many people may have such experience: In freezing winter night you find the light downstairs in your home is still on but don't want to leave the warm bed, on hot summer day you always have to wait for the room to cool down and suffer the swelter when you are back from work. As people's living standard improves and internet gets mature, smart home system is now wanted by increasing number of families. However, technology constrain and high cost prevent traditional smart home system from getting popular. To tackle the problem, Octopus Network LLC combines WIFI and RF technology and come up with an innovative solution.

Nowadays, WIFI is widely used in home, hotel, office and public area. Considered as a fully developed remote control technology, Radio Prequency(RF) is applied in a lot of industries. Due to its characteristics of low cost, long distance communication in all direction, RF is an ideal choice for future smart home. The blend of these two technologies enables control over all the electronic device in your house and bridges it to internet. Therefore, no matter where and when you are, you can operate your home in anyway you want as long as you can connect to internet. It just bring smart home system to a brand new level.

The era of internet of things has arrived, object are becoming intelligent and so does your home. Life would be more efficient and convenient with all these awesome technologies. So please get online and enjoy your new smart home, where your dream becomes true.
